How Etsy Fees Work in 2026

$0.20

Listing Fee

Charged when you publish or renew a listing. Each listing lasts 4 months or until it sells. Applies to both physical and digital products.

6.5%

Transaction Fee

Applied to the total sale amount (item price + shipping). This is Etsy's core marketplace fee for every sale made through the platform.

3% + $0.25

Payment Processing

Etsy Payments processing fee. Covers credit card, debit card, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and other payment methods.

15%

Offsite Ads (Optional)

If a buyer finds you through Etsy's offsite ads (Google, Facebook, etc). Mandatory for shops over $10K/year revenue, optional otherwise.

What percentage does Etsy take from a sale?

Etsy takes a combined 6.5% transaction fee plus a 3% + $0.25 payment processing fee on every sale. Together with the $0.20 listing fee, the effective percentage ranges from about 10% on higher-priced items to 14% on lower-priced items. The fixed costs ($0.20 listing fee and $0.25 processing fee) have a bigger impact on cheaper products.

How much does Etsy take from sellers?

For most sellers, Etsy takes between 10–14% of each sale in combined fees. On a typical $25 sale, you'd pay about $2.83 in fees and keep $22.17. The three mandatory fees are: the $0.20 listing fee (charged upfront when you publish), the 6.5% transaction fee (charged when you sell), and the payment processing fee of 3% + $0.25 (charged when the payment is processed).

How much does it cost to list on Etsy?

It costs $0.20 to list a single item on Etsy. This listing fee is charged when you publish or renew a listing, and each listing stays active for 4 months or until the item sells. If you enable auto-renew, the $0.20 fee is charged again every 4 months. There is no monthly subscription fee to sell on Etsy — you only pay per listing and per sale.

What is Etsy's commission rate?

Etsy's commission (transaction fee) is 6.5% of the total order amount, including shipping. This is separate from the payment processing fee (3% + $0.25) and the listing fee ($0.20). Combined, these fees give Etsy an effective commission of roughly 11.5% on a $25 sale, decreasing slightly as item price increases.

Offsite Ads: If your sale comes through Etsy's offsite advertising (Google, Facebook, Instagram, Pinterest), an additional 15% fee applies, bringing total fees to approximately 26.5%. Shops earning over $10,000/year are automatically enrolled. Shops under that threshold can opt out.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does Etsy charge per listing?

Etsy charges a $0.20 listing fee when you publish or renew a listing. When you make a sale, there's an additional 6.5% transaction fee and a 3% + $0.25 payment processing fee. If the sale came through Etsy's offsite ads, there's an additional 15% fee.

What is Etsy's effective fee rate?

For a typical $25 item without offsite ads, Etsy's total fees come to about $2.88 (11.5% of the sale). With offsite ads, it jumps to about $6.63 (26.5%). Use the calculator above to see your exact effective rate.

Are Etsy offsite ads mandatory?

Offsite ads are optional for shops earning under $10,000/year. For shops above that threshold, Etsy automatically enrolls you in offsite ads at a 15% fee on sales generated through those ads. You only pay the fee when a sale is made.

How do I calculate profit on digital downloads?

Digital downloads have zero shipping and material costs, making profit calculation simpler. Toggle "Digital Download" in the calculator above. Your profit is: sale price minus Etsy fees. At $25, you'd keep about $22.12 per sale.

What price should I set to make a specific profit?

Use the "Price Finder" in the calculator above. Enter your target profit amount and it calculates the selling price needed after all Etsy fees and your costs are deducted.

How much does Etsy shipping cost?

Shipping costs depend on package weight and destination. USPS First Class (under 1 lb) ranges from $3.50-$7.00, while Priority Mail starts at $8-$12 for 1-5 lbs. Etsy offers discounted shipping labels, typically 30% off retail rates. Use our Shipping Calculator tab above.

What is a good Etsy conversion rate?

The average Etsy conversion rate is 1-3%. Top sellers achieve 3-5%, while digital products typically convert at 2-4%. Use our Conversion Benchmarks tab to see how your shop compares.
