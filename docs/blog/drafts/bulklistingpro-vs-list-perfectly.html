BulkListingPro vs List Perfectly: cross-lister vs bulk creator

Two tools that show up in the same Google searches but solve completely different problems. Here's the honest framing.

Michael Smyth
April 18, 2026
TL;DR

List Perfectly is a cross-lister — it takes one listing and posts it to Poshmark, eBay, Mercari, Etsy, Depop, and others. BulkListingPro is a bulk creator — it takes a spreadsheet of products and turns them into new Etsy listings with AI-drafted content. They're not competitors. They're complements for sellers who do both.

Like the Vendoo comparison, this is less a "which should I buy" question and more a "which is for my problem" question. List Perfectly and BulkListingPro both save listing time, but for very different kinds of sellers.

What each does

List Perfectly is built for resellers. You have one vintage jacket, one pair of sneakers, one mid-century lamp — items you want to list on Poshmark and eBay and Mercari and Etsy simultaneously so that whichever marketplace has the buyer first wins the sale. List Perfectly handles the replication and coordination.

BulkListingPro is built for digital product sellers on Etsy. You have fifty new SVG designs, or a hundred planner variations, or a batch of printable wall art — products that live exclusively on Etsy and need to get from a spreadsheet to a live listing as fast as possible. BulkListingPro handles the spreadsheet-to-listing conversion with AI-drafted titles and tags.

Who uses which

  • Resellers (thrift, vintage, designer resale, sneaker flipping) → List Perfectly
  • Digital product sellers (SVG, planners, fonts, templates) → BulkListingPro
  • Handmade sellers with multi-platform strategies → List Perfectly for reach
  • Handmade sellers launching large Etsy drops → BulkListingPro for speed
  • Sellers who do both → both tools, different workflows

The honest recommendation

If you list digital products on Etsy, you want BulkListingPro. If you cross-list physical resale items across marketplaces, you want List Perfectly. If you do both kinds of selling, you use both. There is no head-to-head winner here.

The reason this comparison gets searched is that both tools show up in "Etsy listing tools" search results. But the nanosecond you describe what you're actually trying to do, one of the two becomes obviously right. Ask yourself: am I bulk creating new listings on Etsy, or am I reaching buyers across multiple marketplaces? That answers the question.

Frequently asked questions

Is List Perfectly an Etsy bulk upload tool?

List Perfectly is a cross-listing tool designed to replicate a single listing across multiple marketplaces. It's built for resellers rather than for bulk-creating many new Etsy listings at once.

Can BulkListingPro cross-list to Poshmark or eBay?

No. BulkListingPro is Etsy-only. For cross-listing to Poshmark, eBay, Mercari, or Depop, you need a cross-lister like List Perfectly or Vendoo.

Who is List Perfectly for?

List Perfectly is built for resellers — people selling thrifted, vintage, pre-owned, or designer items that are typically one-of-one. The value prop is getting that single item in front of buyers on multiple marketplaces at once.

Can I use both BulkListingPro and List Perfectly?

Yes. A seller who runs both a digital products shop on Etsy and a resale business across multiple platforms could use BulkListingPro for the Etsy bulk creation and List Perfectly for the cross-listing side.
