BulkListingPro vs V45: bundled creator toolkit vs focused bulk tool

V45 bundles Etsy listing with creator utilities. BulkListingPro does one thing and does it deeply. Who wins for which digital artist?

Michael Smyth
April 18, 2026
TL;DR

V45 (v45.org) is a toolkit for digital artists — Etsy listing uploader plus AI image generator, background remover, pattern maker, and shop name generator — all bundled together. BulkListingPro focuses narrowly on bulk listing with AI-drafted content. Pick V45 if you want the art-creation tools bundled in the same place as the listing tool. Pick BulkListingPro if you already have your artwork and want the deepest listing-specific workflow.

V45 and BulkListingPro both target Etsy digital product sellers — specifically digital artists launching batches of products. They overlap on "we help you list faster" but diverge sharply on how wide the toolkit spans and how deep the listing workflow goes.

This one's a real head-to-head for the same audience. The choice is about whether you want an all-in-one art-and-listing toolkit or a deeper, listing-specific tool that does that one job thoroughly.

What each tool covers

V45 (v45.org) is a bundled suite of automation tools for digital artists. Its headline product is the Etsy listing creator and uploader, and it sits alongside:

  • AI image generator — create art from prompts
  • Background remover — clean up existing images
  • Pattern maker — generate tileable patterns
  • Shop name generator — idea generation for new shops

The positioning: you're a digital artist who wants to make art and list it, all in one toolkit. V45 tries to span both jobs.

BulkListingPro is narrower by design. It does one thing — turn a CSV of products into live Etsy listings with AI-drafted titles, tags, and descriptions — and invests its depth there. No image generator. No shop name utility. Just the listing workflow, built thoroughly.

The philosophical split

The tools make opposite bets:

V45's bet: digital artists benefit from having art-creation tools and listing tools in the same place. The integration between "made the art" and "listed the art" is valuable.

BulkListingPro's bet: most digital sellers already have their art-creation workflow figured out (Affinity, Canva, Photoshop, or wherever). The bottleneck is the listing side specifically, and going deep on that one workflow beats a broader surface.

Neither bet is wrong. Which one works for you depends on whether your art-creation pipeline is already settled.

Who should pick which

Pick V45 if:

  • You're a digital artist who also wants AI art generation as part of the same toolkit
  • You need occasional background removal and pattern generation
  • You're starting a new shop and the shop name generator sounds useful
  • You value having multiple creator utilities in one place over having the deepest possible listing workflow

Pick BulkListingPro if:

  • Your art creation workflow is already settled (other tools handle it)
  • Your bottleneck is specifically the Etsy listing side, not the art side
  • You want AI-drafted titles, tags, and descriptions per listing with a deep editor (form view, grid view, tag library, SEO evaluation)
  • Transparent credit pricing ($1.99–$24.99 packs, no subscription) matters to you

Use both if:

  • You want V45's art generation utilities but find its listing workflow too shallow for serious drops
  • Use V45 for image generation and background removal, then export and import the images into BulkListingPro for the listing pipeline
The real question isn't "which tool." It's "what do you already have?" If your art pipeline is done and you need listing speed, BulkListingPro goes deeper. If you want to generate art too, V45 bundles that in.

The verdict

V45 is a reasonable fit for digital artists who want a single tool for both art generation and Etsy listing. BulkListingPro is the better fit for sellers whose listing workflow is the specific, isolated bottleneck and who want that workflow to be as thorough as possible — with an AI-drafted content layer, grid and form editors, per-listing credit pricing, and a deep validator.

If you're deciding between them, match the tool to where your time currently goes. If you burn hours on image creation, V45's bundle might matter. If you burn hours on listing itself, BulkListingPro's depth on that one job is the reason it exists.

Frequently asked questions

What is V45?

V45 (v45.org) is a suite of automation tools for digital artists selling on Etsy. It combines an Etsy listing creator and uploader with additional creator tools — an AI image generator, background remover, pattern maker, and shop name generator.

Does V45 use AI?

Yes. V45's image generator is an AI tool for creating art imagery. Its listing uploader automates the form-filling side of Etsy listing creation.

Which tool is better for bulk listing specifically?

If you already have your artwork and just need efficient listing, BulkListingPro. If you also want an AI art generator as part of the same toolkit, V45 bundles both.

Which is cheaper?

BulkListingPro's pricing is transparent: credit packs from $1.99 to $24.99 with no subscription. V45's specific pricing should be verified directly on v45.org before comparing.
