Top 10 Best Selling Digital Product Categories on Etsy

These are the top selling products on Etsy for digital downloads, ranked by sales volume and search demand. Whether you're just starting or scaling, these are the things that sell well on Etsy right now.

🖼
#1

Printable Wall Art & Posters

The single largest digital product category on Etsy. Buyers love instant-download art for nurseries, living rooms, and offices. Minimalist and boho styles dominate. Zero fulfillment cost means nearly pure profit on every sale.

Avg Price: $3 - $15 Search Demand: 14,800+/mo

Tip: Offer bundles (3-5 prints) at a discount to increase average order value.

✄
#2

SVG Cut Files (Cricut/Silhouette)

Massive demand from the crafting community. SVG files for t-shirts, mugs, tumblers, and home decor sell year-round with spikes around holidays. Sellers with 200+ designs build recurring passive income streams.

Avg Price: $1.50 - $8 Search Demand: 12,100+/mo

Tip: Include multiple file formats (SVG, PNG, DXF, EPS) to maximize perceived value.

📅
#3

Digital Planners & Organizers

GoodNotes and Notability planners are an Etsy trending category. Buyers want hyperlinked, customizable planners for productivity, fitness, budgeting, and meal planning. Seasonal releases (academic year, Q1 goal setting) drive spikes.

Avg Price: $5 - $15 Search Demand: 9,900+/mo

Tip: Create variations (undated, dated, academic year) and list each as a separate listing for more visibility.

📄
#4

Canva Templates (Social Media, Resumes)

Canva templates are Etsy best sellers because buyers can customize them without design skills. Instagram post templates, resume templates, media kits, and business card designs lead the category. Small business owners are the primary buyers.

Avg Price: $5 - $25 Search Demand: 8,100+/mo

Tip: Niche down (e.g., "real estate agent Instagram templates") for less competition and higher conversion.

💍
#5

Wedding Invitations & Stationery

Editable wedding invitation suites are among the most popular Etsy products. Buyers want matching sets: invitation, RSVP, details card, menu, and program. Canva and Templett-based templates dominate. High perceived value enables premium pricing.

Avg Price: $10 - $40 Search Demand: 6,600+/mo

Tip: List each piece in the suite individually AND as a bundle to maximize listing count and search presence.

💼
#6

Business Document Templates (LLC, Legal)

LLC operating agreements, invoices, contracts, non-disclosure agreements, and business plan templates. Buyers are small business owners and freelancers who want professional documents without lawyer fees. High urgency purchases.

Avg Price: $8 - $35 Search Demand: 5,400+/mo

Tip: Include clear instructions and examples. Buyers value professionalism and completeness in legal templates.

📚
#7

Educational Worksheets & Flashcards

Homeschool and classroom resources are an Etsy trending now category. Printable worksheets, flashcards, activity pages, and curriculum bundles. Demand peaks during back-to-school season but remains steady year-round from homeschooling families.

Avg Price: $3 - $12 Search Demand: 4,400+/mo

Tip: Target specific grade levels and subjects. "3rd grade multiplication worksheets" converts better than generic math worksheets.

🧵
#8

Embroidery & Sewing Patterns

Machine embroidery designs (PES, DST, JEF formats) and hand embroidery PDF patterns. The crafting community on Etsy is loyal and repeat-purchase heavy. Sellers often build large catalogs of 500+ designs across themes and holidays.

Avg Price: $2 - $10 Search Demand: 3,600+/mo

Tip: Provide multiple hoop sizes and format options. Stitch count and color charts boost buyer confidence.

📷
#9

Lightroom Presets & Photo Overlays

Photographers and Instagram influencers buy presets for consistent photo editing. Lightroom mobile and desktop presets, Photoshop actions, and seasonal photo overlays (snow, bokeh, confetti). Niche aesthetics like "moody," "film," or "bright and airy" sell best.

Avg Price: $4 - $20 Search Demand: 2,900+/mo

Tip: Show dramatic before/after photos in your listing images. Include mobile AND desktop versions.

🔠
#10

Fonts & Graphic Design Assets

Custom fonts, clipart bundles, texture packs, and design element collections. Buyers are designers, crafters, and small business owners building brands. Font bundles with commercial licenses command premium prices.

Avg Price: $5 - $25 Search Demand: 2,400+/mo

Tip: Always include a commercial license option. Show your font in real-world mockups (logos, packaging, wedding invites).

How to Start Selling Digital Products on Etsy

Whether you want to sell printables, templates, or SVG files, here's how to sell digital downloads on Etsy step by step.

1

Create Your Digital Product

Use tools like Canva, Adobe Illustrator, Procreate, or Affinity Designer. Focus on a niche you know well. Quality and consistency matter more than quantity when you're starting out.

2

Set Up Your Etsy Shop

Creating an Etsy shop is free. Choose a memorable shop name, write a compelling "About" section, and set up your shop policies. Enable Etsy Payments to accept all major payment methods.

3

Optimize Your Listing (Title, Tags, Description)

Use all 13 tags. Put primary keywords at the beginning of your title. Write descriptions that include long-tail search terms naturally. Etsy SEO is the #1 driver of organic sales for digital products.

4

Price Competitively

Research competing listings in your category. Digital products have near-zero cost, so your pricing strategy should balance volume vs. perceived value. Use our Etsy fee calculator to understand your real profit margins.
